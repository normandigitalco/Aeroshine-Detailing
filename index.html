Why Aeroshine

Quality Focused

San Diego streets are rough on paint — brake dust, sea mist, UV glare, and freeway grime build up fast. Staying on top of it is the difference between a car that holds its value and one that doesn't.

We Come to You

Skip the drop-off, the waiting room, and the time out of your day. We show up with everything needed and work around your schedule — driveway, parking spot, or office lot.

Hand Wash Every Time

Tunnel washes drag dirt across your clear coat every single time. Every Aeroshine wash starts with a foam pre-soak and a proper hand wash — no brushes, no swirl marks, no shortcuts.

Cars, SUVs, Trucks & RVs

Daily drivers, exotic sports cars, lifted trucks, and full-size RVs — we've done them all. No job is too big or too clean. Every vehicle gets the same level of attention.

Protect Your Investment

A detail isn't just about looking good today. Every service includes a protection step so your paint, trim, and interior stay cleaner longer — and your car holds its value.

Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking your detail.

Text 619-404-7007 or DM us on Instagram @aeroshinedetailingllc. We'll lock in a time that works for you and answer any questions about your vehicle upfront. No booking portal — we keep it direct.
Fully mobile — your driveway, your parking spot, your office lot. We haul everything ourselves. Just have the car accessible and give us room to work around it.
We carry our own water supply and equipment for most services. For certain locations or longer jobs, access to a nearby outdoor spigot is helpful but not always required. Let us know your situation when booking and we'll coordinate accordingly.
Exterior only runs about 1.5 to 2 hours. Interior detail is 2 to 3 hours. A full inside-and-out can run 3 to 5 hours on larger vehicles or heavily soiled ones. We'll give you a realistic window when you book — no surprises.
We detail Cars, SUVs, Trucks, and RVs. Sedans, coupes, crossovers, pickup trucks, minivans — if it has wheels, we can detail it. We regularly work on luxury and performance vehicles including Mercedes, BMW, Tesla, Dodge, and Ford.
Our maintenance plans are recurring monthly or bi-weekly detail appointments at a discounted member rate. They're ideal for people who want to keep their vehicle consistently clean without thinking about scheduling. Members also get priority booking over one-time clients. Text us to get started.
Pricing is based on vehicle size (sedan, SUV/truck, oversized/RV) and current condition. A heavily soiled interior or heavily contaminated paint takes more time and product. We always confirm pricing before starting and are upfront about any additional charges.
We serve the greater San Diego area including Chula Vista, National City, La Mesa, El Cajon, Santee, and surrounding communities. Not sure if we cover your area? Just text us your zip code and we'll let you know.
